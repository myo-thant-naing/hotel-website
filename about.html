About

Bagan Thiripyistaya Sanctuary Resort is situated on the banks of the Irrawaddy, Myanmar's river of history and legend. The Resort sprawls over 24 acres of landscaped gardens which is home to mature tropical trees and flowering plants, as well as native birds and a number of small tame animals.Oriented towards the west, the Resort revels in panoramic river views, glorious sunsets and silver moon glow, and a pervading atmosphere of profound peace, warmth and comfort.
